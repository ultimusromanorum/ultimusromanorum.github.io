Ultimus Romanorum

Authors to read for learning classical rhetoric

  1. Aristotle: Rhetorica.
  2. Cicero: Rhetorica ad Herennium. De Oratore. De Partitione Oratoria. De Inventione. Brutus. Topica.
  3. Seneca Maior: Suasoriae. Controversiae.
  4. Quintilian: Institutio Oratoria. Declamationes.
  5. Calpurnius Flaccus: Declamationes.
  6. Aelius Theon: Progymnasmata
  7. Hermogenes: Περὶ τῶν στάσεων. Περὶ εὺρέσεως. Περὶ ιδεῶν.
  8. Libanius: Progymnasmata.
  9. Aphthonius: Progymnasmata.
  10. Nicolaus: Progymnasmata
  11. Erasmus: De Utraque Verborum ac Rerum Copia
  12. Hugh Blair: Lectures on Rhetoric and Belles Lettres
